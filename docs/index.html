The developer bank

Welcome to 51 Bank. A platform where you can create customers, manage accounts, move money, and generally have fun with payments !!!

Get Started or Start Playing

Nearly a real bank, huh ?

No. Because all your customers, accounts, payments live only in your browser. When you are out nothing happens but when you are back you can carry on from where you left off

So, What's the point ?

Because, although it's not real it's been built like if it was. I am a developer and I wanted to craft an API from a developer's point of view.

So, who is it for ?

It's for every developer in payments. For those who are moving the first steps in the payment world and they might just want to use it to learn more but without getting confused by legacy systems, outdated middleware, licenses to sign , builds to run, bloated API, ... For those who working on other APIs to use it as a model for a constructive comparison (as a springboard for critical thought and deep understanding) For those tasked to design API for their payment systems to use it as a blueprint or a springboard for new ideas
You need to know the very basics of CURL, understand a REST API and a few tiny concepts of the payment world
